Q1) What is cloud Computing?
Cloud computing is the delivery of different services through the Internet., storage, databases, networking,
 software, analytics, and intelligence—over the Internet (“the cloud”) to offer flexible resources, and economies of scale.
 You typically pay only for cloud services you use, helping you lower your operating costs,
 run your infrastructure more efficiently, and scale as your business needs change.
