Q1. What is cloud Computing?
Ans: Cloud computing is the delivery of on-demand computing services
 -- from applications to storage and processing power -- typically over the internet and on a pay-as-you-go basis.
 
Q2. What are the different types of Cloud?
Ans: There are four different types of clouds:
    1. Public Cloud
    2. Private Cloud
    3. Hybrid Cloud
    4. Community Cloud
